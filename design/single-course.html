Course Overview

This BBS-approved continuing education course provides California mental health professionals with a comprehensive review of the laws, regulations, and ethical standards governing clinical practice. Through real-world vignettes and BBS-relevant case examples, you'll strengthen your ability to make sound ethical decisions in everyday practice settings.

Content covers scope of practice, mandated reporting, confidentiality, professional boundaries, and recent regulatory updates affecting LMFTs, LCSWs, LPCCs, and LEPs. Upon successful completion of the course exam, your CE certificate is generated automatically — ready to document for licensure renewal.

What You'll Learn In This Course:

  • Identify California BBS scope-of-practice requirements for LMFTs, LCSWs, LPCCs, and LEPs.
  • Apply mandated reporting laws and ethical decision-making frameworks in complex clinical cases.
  • Navigate dual relationships, confidentiality limits, and Tarasoff duty-to-warn obligations.
  • Evaluate advertising, telehealth, and documentation standards under current California law.
  • Develop a personal ethics compliance plan aligned with BBS regulations and professional standards.
