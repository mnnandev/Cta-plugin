Supervision FAQ

CTA provides supervision for registered associates working toward licensure as AMFTs, ASWs, and APCCs in California. You must be registered with the BBS and have a signed supervision agreement on file before your first session.
Group supervision is billed as a monthly subscription at $260/month, which includes four 2-hour group sessions. Individual supervision is pay-per-session at $120 for each 60-minute appointment. You can manage billing and receipts from your account dashboard.
Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance through your dashboard. Missed group sessions cannot be refunded but may be made up during an alternate slot when available. Individual sessions canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be charged in full.
Yes. All supervision is provided by Candice Fuimaono, MS, LMFT — a BBS-approved supervisor. Sessions meet California requirements for associate supervision, including documentation, hour tracking, and the 8-person group size cap mandated by state law.
