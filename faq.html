FAQ by Category

CE Courses

Your required CE topics depend on your California BBS license type (LMFT, LCSW, LPCC, or LEP) and renewal cycle. Check the BBS continuing education requirements for your license, then use our course catalog filters to find courses tagged with the categories you still need — such as law and ethics, cultural diversity, or suicide risk assessment. If you're unsure, contact us with your license number and renewal date and we'll recommend a compliant plan.
Most individual CE courses remain available in your dashboard for 12 months from the date of purchase, giving you plenty of time to work at your own pace around your caseload. Annual All-Access members can complete included courses anytime during their active subscription year. Progress is saved automatically, so you can pause mid-module and return later without losing your place.
You must score at least 70% on the final exam to earn CE credit, per BBS guidelines. If you don't pass on your first attempt, you can review the course material and retake the exam — most courses allow up to three attempts at no additional charge. After exhausting retakes, contact support and we'll help you determine whether a course reset or alternative option is appropriate.
Yes. Clinical Training and Supervision Academy is an approved continuing education provider recognized by the California Board of Behavioral Sciences. Each course listing shows the provider number, CE hours awarded, and applicable license categories. Certificates generated upon passing include all information required for BBS license renewal documentation.

Supervision

Individual supervision is a one-on-one session focused entirely on your cases, documentation, and professional development. Group supervision brings together a small cohort of associates under one supervisor, allowing you to learn from peers' cases while meeting BBS group supervision requirements. Both formats count toward your weekly supervision hours when documented correctly; the best fit depends on your learning style, schedule, and where you are in your associate registration.
Our group supervision sessions are capped at six associates per supervisor, in line with California BBS limits for group supervision. This keeps discussions focused and ensures every participant has time to present cases and receive feedback. Session rosters are managed through Amelia scheduling so you always know who will be in your group before you book.
Yes. Many associates combine group and individual supervision to balance cost efficiency with personalized guidance — for example, weekly group sessions plus monthly individual check-ins. You can book either format through your dashboard at any time, subject to supervisor availability. We recommend discussing your ratio with your supervisor so your documented hours align with BBS requirements for your registration category.
Before your first supervision session, upload a signed Supervision Agreement, proof of current associate registration with the BBS, and liability insurance verification if required by your site. You'll also submit a brief case summary or treatment plan for any client you plan to discuss. All documents are stored securely in your supervision dashboard and can be updated as your caseload or employment changes.

Billing

One-time course and bundle purchases are charged in full at checkout through Stripe. Subscription plans — such as the Annual All-Access Pass or Supervision + CE Hybrid — renew automatically on your billing date each month or year. You'll receive an email receipt for every charge, and invoices are available for download from your account billing history at any time.
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled from your dashboard under Account Settings — cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period, and you'll retain access until that date. Annual plans can also be canceled before renewal; we don't auto-renew without notice. One-time course purchases are not subscriptions and do not require cancellation.
If a renewal payment fails, Stripe will retry the charge over the next several days and we'll email you to update your card on file. Your access remains active during a short grace period so you're not locked out mid-course. If payment isn't resolved after the grace period, subscription benefits pause until the balance is cleared — previously completed certificates remain in your dashboard.

Certificates

Your CE certificate is generated automatically within a few minutes of passing the final course exam with a score of 70% or higher. You'll see a download link on the completion screen and in your dashboard under Completed Courses. An email confirmation with a PDF attachment is also sent to the address on your account for your records.
Each certificate includes your full legal name, course title, number of CE hours, completion date, applicable BBS license categories, and our approved provider number. It also lists the instructor name and a brief course description as required for audit purposes. Verify your name spelling in Account Settings before completing exams — certificates use the name on file at the time of completion.
Yes. All certificates for courses you've completed remain available in your dashboard indefinitely under the Certificates or Completed Courses section. You can re-download PDF copies whenever you need them for BBS renewal, employer verification, or personal records. If you update your legal name after completion, contact support to request a reissued certificate with corrected information.

Technical

Our course player works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Enable JavaScript and allow cookies for ctacademy.org so progress tracking and quiz submissions work correctly. If you experience playback issues, try clearing your browser cache or switching to a supported browser before contacting support.
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter the email address associated with your account. You'll receive a secure reset link within a few minutes — check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive. Links expire after 24 hours for security; request a new one if needed. Still locked out? Contact us from the email on your account and we'll verify your identity manually.
Make sure you're logged into the same email address used at checkout — purchases are tied to your account, not a device. If you recently upgraded a membership, allow a few minutes for access to sync or try logging out and back in. For failed payments or expired subscriptions, update your billing info under Account Settings; contact support with your order confirmation if access still doesn't appear.
